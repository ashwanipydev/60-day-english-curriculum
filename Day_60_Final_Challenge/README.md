## Your Day 60 Final Challenge

Record a **5-minute video presentation** on this topic:

> **"Who am I, what do I do, and what are my plans?"**

This is your graduation test. No script. No notes. Just you and the camera.

**Cover:**
1. **Introduction** (1 min) — Name, background, current role
2. **What you do** (1 min) — Your work, responsibilities, team
3. **Your strengths** (1 min) — 3 skills + one achievement
4. **Your goals** (1 min) — Short-term and long-term career plans
5. **Your English journey** (1 min) — What you started with, what improved, what's next
