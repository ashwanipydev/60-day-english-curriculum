# Day 17 — Speaking Drills: Building Fluency

## The Speaking Problem

Most Hindi speakers know grammar but freeze when they need to speak. Why?

1. **Translation habit** — thinking in Hindi first, translating to English
2. **Fear of mistakes** — waiting until the sentence is perfect before speaking
3. **No speaking practice** — reading and writing English but never speaking

---

## Drill 1: Sentence Continuation (5 min daily)

Start a sentence and don't stop. If you get stuck, use fillers:

**Fillers to use:**
- "What I mean is..."
- "Let me think about that..."
- "In other words..."
- "So basically..."

Start with: "Today at work, I..." and keep going for 2 minutes without stopping.

---

## Drill 2: The Repeat-After-Me Drill (10 min)

Find a YouTube video with subtitles. Pause after every sentence. Repeat it **exactly** — same rhythm, same stress, same speed.

Do this with the same 2-minute clip every day for a week. You'll naturally absorb the rhythm of English.

---

## Drill 3: Describe Everything (5 min)

While doing something routine (making tea, commuting, washing up) — describe it in English out loud:

> "I'm making chai. I'm adding water to the pan. Now I'm putting it on the stove. The water is starting to boil..."

This builds the habit of **thinking in English in real time**.

---

## Drill 4: The 60-Second Talk

Pick a topic. Speak for exactly 60 seconds without stopping.

**Week 3 topics:**
- My morning routine
- My work tasks today
- My team
- My favorite food
- My city

Don't worry about mistakes. Fluency first, accuracy second.

---

## Common Hindi-Speaker Speaking Errors

| Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|
| "I am not understanding." | "I don't understand." |
| "She is knowing the answer." | "She knows the answer." |
| "I am having a meeting." | "I have a meeting." *(for schedule)* |
| "He is liking cricket." | "He likes cricket." |

> Verbs like **know, like, have, understand, belong** are NOT used in continuous form.

---

## Speaking Task

Do all 4 drills today. Time yourself. Keep a log of how long you can speak without pausing.
