## Today's Speaking Challenge

> Call a friend and speak only English for 5 minutes

---

## Phone Call English

Phone calls are harder than face-to-face because you can't see facial expressions or lip movements.

---

## Starting a Call

**You are calling:**
> "Good morning, this is [Name] calling from [company]. May I speak with [person]?"
> "Hi [Name], this is [Your Name]. Do you have a minute to talk?"

**You receive a call:**
> "Good morning, [Name] speaking."
> "Hello, [company name], how can I help you?"

---

## During the Call

| Situation | Phrase |
|-----------|--------|
| Didn't hear | "I'm sorry, could you repeat that?" |
| Bad connection | "I'm sorry, I'm having trouble hearing you. Could you speak a little louder?" |
| Confirming | "Just to confirm, you said [X], correct?" |
| Taking notes | "Could you please spell that for me?" |
| Putting on hold | "Could you hold for just a moment?" |

---

## Ending a Call Professionally

> "Thank you so much for your time. I'll follow up with an email."
> "That was very helpful. I'll get started on this right away."
> "Thank you. Have a great rest of your day!"
> "I'll send you the details over email. Speak soon!"

---

## Video Call Phrases

| Situation | Phrase |
|-----------|--------|
| You were on mute | "Sorry, I was on mute. What I was saying is..." |
| Connectivity issue | "I'm having some connectivity issues. Can everyone still hear me?" |
| Sharing screen | "Can everyone see my screen?" |
| Previous slide | "Could we go back to the previous slide?" |
| Joining late | "I apologize for joining late. Could someone briefly catch me up?" |

---

## Speaking Task

Today's challenge: Call a friend, family member, or colleague and speak **only English** for 5 minutes. If no one is available, record a 5-minute "phone call practice" talking to yourself.
