## Day 50 Assessment: The Professional Video

Record a **3-minute professional "About Me" video**. Treat it like a LinkedIn intro or conference presentation.

**Include:**
1. Who you are and your role (30 seconds)
2. Your experience and key skills (45 seconds)
3. A recent project or achievement (45 seconds)
4. What you're working on now and your goals (30 seconds)
5. What you bring to a team or project (30 seconds)

**This is your professional voice. Make it polished.**
