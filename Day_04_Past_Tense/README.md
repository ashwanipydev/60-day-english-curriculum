## Two Past Tenses

### Simple Past = It happened, it's over

- **Formula:** Subject + **V2** (past form)
- **Use:** Completed action in the past

| Example | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| I attended the meeting. | मैंने meeting attend की। |
| She sent the email. | उसने email भेजी। |
| We completed the project. | हमने project पूरा किया। |

### Past Continuous = Was happening at a specific time

- **Formula:** Subject + **was/were** + V**+ing**
- **Use:** An action that was in progress at a certain point

| Example | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| I was working when he called. | जब उसने call किया, मैं काम कर रहा था। |
| She was presenting when the client arrived. | जब client आया, वो present कर रही थी। |

## 20 Essential V2 Forms (Memorize These)

| V1 (Present) | V2 (Past) | V1 (Present) | V2 (Past) |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|-----------|
| go | **went** | say | **said** |
| give | **gave** | come | **came** |
| take | **took** | make | **made** |
| tell | **told** | write | **wrote** |
| send | **sent** | meet | **met** |
| speak | **spoke** | read | **read** |
| see | **saw** | know | **knew** |
| think | **thought** | get | **got** |
| have | **had** | do | **did** |
| begin | **began** | finish | **finished** |

> For a full list of 75 V2 forms, see [grammar/v2-verb-forms.md](../grammar/v2-verb-forms.md)

## Practice

Fill in the correct past tense:

1. She _____ (send) the report yesterday.
2. I _____ (work) on the project when the power went out.
3. They _____ (attend) the conference last week.
4. He _____ (write) the email this morning.
5. We _____ (have) a meeting at 3 PM.

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. sent
2. was working
3. attended
4. wrote
5. had

</details>

## Speaking Task

Talk about your **yesterday** — what did you do? **10 sentences minimum.**

Start each sentence with: "Yesterday, I..." or "Last night, I..." or "In the morning, I..."

---

## Key Takeaway

> Simple Past = finished action (V2). Past Continuous = was happening (was/were + V-ing). Both need V2 forms — memorize the top 20.
