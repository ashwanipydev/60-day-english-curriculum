## 5 Key Conversation Topics Every Professional Needs

Being able to speak on these 5 topics will cover 90% of your daily office conversations.

---

### Topic 1: Your Job / Role

Practice answering these questions:
- "What do you do?"
- "What does your role involve?"
- "What team are you in?"

**Script template:**
> "I work as a [role] at [company]. I am part of the [team/department] team. My main responsibilities include [3 tasks]. I've been in this role for [duration]."

---

### Topic 2: Your Projects / Work

Practice answering:
- "What are you working on?"
- "What's your current project?"

**Script template:**
> "Currently, I'm working on [project name]. The goal is to [purpose]. We're expected to complete it by [date]. I'm responsible for [your part]."

---

### Topic 3: Your Team and Work Culture

Practice:
- "How big is your team?"
- "What's it like working there?"

**Script template:**
> "My team has about [number] people. We work [remotely/in office/hybrid]. The culture is [describe]. I report to [manager title]."

---

### Topic 4: Your Goals

Practice:
- "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?"
- "What are you hoping to achieve?"

**Script template:**
> "In the next few years, I'd like to [goal]. I'm currently building skills in [area]. Eventually, I hope to [longer-term goal]."

---

### Topic 5: Small Talk Topics

Practice natural small talk:
- Weather: "The weather is quite nice today, isn't it?"
- Weekend: "How was your weekend? Did you do anything interesting?"
- Work: "How's the project coming along?"
- News: "Did you hear about [topic]?"

---

## Practice Task

Prepare a 2-minute answer for each of the 5 topics. Practice them out loud. Time yourself.

---

## Speaking Task

Have a full 5-minute "conversation" with yourself — switch between asking and answering questions across all 5 topics.
