## Phase 3: Professional English (Days 31–50)

From today, every day has a **speaking challenge** — one real task to push your comfort zone.

This is where English becomes **yours**. Not theory. Not exercises. Real usage.

---

## Today's Focus: The Professional Introduction Video

Record a **2-minute introduction video** as if you were introducing yourself at a new job or conference.

**Include:**
- Your name and role
- Your experience and key skills
- A current project or goal
- Why you're excited about your work

**Tips:**
- Look at the camera, not the screen
- Stand straight — body language matters
- Speak naturally, not robotically
- It's okay to do 2–3 takes

---

## Evaluation Checklist

After recording, check:
- [ ] Did I maintain eye contact with the camera?
- [ ] Was my speed natural (not too fast, not too slow)?
- [ ] Did I use varied vocabulary (not repeating the same words)?
- [ ] Did I sound confident?
- [ ] Was the pronunciation clear?

---

## Phrases to Use Today

- "I'm excited to share a bit about myself..."
- "My background is in [field]..."
- "Currently, I'm working on..."
- "What drives me is..."
- "I'm looking forward to..."

---

## Key Takeaway

> The speaking challenge is NOT about perfection. It's about doing it. Every recording is progress, even if it feels uncomfortable.
