## Revision Exercise 1: Phrase Challenge

Without looking at your notes, use these 10 phrases in conversation:

1. "That makes sense."
2. "I'll follow up on that."
3. "Could you please clarify?"
4. "We're on track."
5. "Let me think about that."
6. "I'll get back to you."
7. "Happy to help!"
8. "There's been a slight delay."
9. "Let's circle back on this."
10. "Could you loop in [Name]?"

---

## Revision Exercise 2: Speaking Test

Set a timer for 3 minutes. Speak continuously on this topic:

> "Describe your typical workday from morning to evening. Include: what time you start, what tasks you do, any meetings, challenges, and how you end the day."

---

## Key Self-Assessment Questions

1. Can I hold a simple 2-minute conversation without stopping?
2. Do I understand 60–70% of English YouTube videos I watch?
3. Am I using English phrases naturally (not translating from Hindi every time)?
4. Have I reduced my major pronunciation errors?
