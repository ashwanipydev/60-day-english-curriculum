## What You Covered This Week

| Day | Topic | Core Rule |
|-----|-------|-----------|
| Day 1 | Sentence Structure | S + V + O (verb comes second) |
| Day 2 | Verbs | Never skip am/is/are |
| Day 3 | Present Tense | Simple = habit, Continuous = right now |
| Day 4 | Past Tense | V2 for simple past, was/were+ing for continuous |
| Day 5 | Future Tense | will / going to / Present Continuous |
| Day 6 | Questions | Helper verb before subject |

---

## Revision Checklist

Before moving on, make sure you can do all of these:

- [ ] Form S+V+O sentences naturally
- [ ] Use am/is/are without forgetting
- [ ] Switch between Simple and Continuous present
- [ ] Use 20 essential V2 verb forms correctly
- [ ] Talk about future with 3 different forms
- [ ] Form Yes/No and WH questions correctly

---

## Morning Task (30 min)

Review Days 1–6 notes. Focus on the rules you still feel unsure about.

---

## Afternoon Task (45 min): Mock Self-Introduction

Speak this script out loud, using all the tenses:

> "My name is [Name]. I am a [designation] at [company]. I work in [department] and I handle [your tasks]. Yesterday, I [something you did at work]. Tomorrow, I am going to [your plan]. This week, I have been working on [project]."

Do this **5 times** — improve each time.

---

## Evening Task (45 min): Write a Short Paragraph

Write a short paragraph about your week using all tenses you learned:

**Template:**
> "This week was [adjective]. On Monday, I [past — what you did]. Currently, I am [present continuous — what's in progress]. Tomorrow, I will [future plan]. I usually [simple present — your routine]. My biggest challenge was [something hard], but I [how you handled it]."

---

## Speaking Challenge

Record yourself answering these questions (1–2 minutes each):

1. "Tell me about yourself."
2. "What did you do at work yesterday?"
3. "What are your plans for next week?"

Listen back. Count your errors. Don't judge — just notice.
