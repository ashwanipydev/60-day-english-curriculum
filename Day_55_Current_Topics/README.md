## Today's Speaking Challenge

> Give your opinion on a current topic (tech/work/life)

---

## Speaking on Trending Topics

Being able to speak on current events — tech, business, society, work culture — is a sign of fluency and engagement.

---

## Opinion Structure for Any Topic

1. **State your position:** "I think/believe/feel that..."
2. **Give a reason:** "because / since / given that..."
3. **Give an example:** "For instance... / For example..."
4. **Acknowledge the other side:** "While some might argue... / I understand that..."
5. **Conclude your position:** "However, overall I think..."

---

## Current Topic Ideas

Pick one and speak for 2 minutes:

**Work & Career:**
- "Do you think remote work is better than office work?"
- "Should companies invest more in employee mental health?"
- "Is a 4-day work week a good idea?"

**Technology:**
- "How is AI changing your industry?"
- "Are social media platforms good or bad for professionals?"
- "What's the most important tech skill for the future?"

**Life & Society:**
- "What's one thing that should change about Indian workplaces?"
- "How important is work-life balance, really?"
- "What skill do you think schools should teach more of?"

---

## Disagreeing on a Topic

This is advanced: disagreeing clearly AND respectfully.

> "I see why many people think [X], and there's merit in that view. However, I'd argue that [Y] because [reason]. In my experience, [example]. So while [X] has its place, I'm more inclined towards [Y]."

---

## Speaking Task

Choose one of the topic questions above. Record a **2-minute spoken opinion** using the full opinion structure. Don't script it — just organize your thoughts and speak.
