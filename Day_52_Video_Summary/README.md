## Taking Your Summarizing Skills to the Next Level

By now you can do the 5-sentence summary. Today we add **critical thinking** to your summaries.

---

## The Advanced Summary Framework

1. **What:** What is the main topic?
2. **Why:** Why does this matter?
3. **Key points:** What are the 3 most important ideas?
4. **Agree/Disagree:** What do you think about it?
5. **Apply:** How does this connect to your life or work?

---

## Critical Thinking Phrases

**Agreeing with an idea:**
- "This supports the idea that..."
- "This confirms what I've observed..."
- "I found this particularly compelling because..."

**Questioning an idea:**
- "However, one could argue that..."
- "The weakness in this argument is..."
- "I'm not fully convinced because..."

**Connecting to real life:**
- "This applies to my work because..."
- "In my experience, this is [true/not always true]..."
- "A real-world example of this is..."
