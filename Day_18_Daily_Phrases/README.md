# Day 18 — 50 Most-Used Daily English Phrases

---

## Why Learn Phrases, Not Just Words

Individual words don't make you fluent — **phrases do**.

Native speakers don't think of individual words. They use pre-built phrase chunks.
Learn these 50 phrases so they come out automatically.

---

## Starting and Ending a Day

1. "Good morning, how are you doing?"
2. "Have a good one!"
3. "Talk to you later."
4. "I'll catch up with you tomorrow."
5. "Take care!"

## Asking for Help / Information

6. "Could you help me with something?"
7. "Do you have a minute?"
8. "Can I ask you something quickly?"
9. "I'm not sure about this — could you clarify?"
10. "Would you mind explaining that again?"

## Agreeing and Disagreeing

11. "That makes sense."
12. "I completely agree."
13. "Absolutely!"
14. "Fair point."
15. "I see your point, but..."
16. "I'm not sure I agree with that."
17. "That's an interesting perspective."
18. "I respectfully disagree."

## Clarifying What You Said

19. "What I mean is..."
20. "Let me rephrase that."
21. "In other words..."
22. "To put it simply..."
23. "What I'm trying to say is..."

## Buying Time / Thinking

24. "Let me think about that for a moment."
25. "That's a good question."
26. "Give me a second to process that."
27. "I'll have to get back to you on that."
28. "Let me check and confirm."

## Giving Updates

29. "Just to keep you in the loop..."
30. "Quick update on this..."
31. "We're on track."
32. "There's been a slight delay."
33. "Everything is going as planned."

## Making Requests

34. "Would it be possible to...?"
35. "Could you please...?"
36. "I'd appreciate it if you could..."
37. "When you get a chance, could you...?"
38. "No rush, but could you...?"

## Offering Help

39. "Let me know if you need anything."
40. "Happy to help!"
41. "I can take that on."
42. "Leave it with me."
43. "I'll handle it."

## Small Talk

44. "How was your weekend?"
45. "Busy day?"
46. "How's the project going?"
47. "Did you catch the match?"
48. "Any plans for the weekend?"

## Wrapping Up

49. "Let's circle back on this."
50. "I'll follow up with you."

---

## Practice Challenge

Use **at least 10** of these phrases naturally in conversation or your speaking drills today. Don't just read them — say them out loud 3 times each.
