## Week 4 Goal Check

By end of Week 4, you should be able to:
- [ ] Hold a 5-minute conversation without major pauses
- [ ] Introduce yourself clearly in 3 different versions
- [ ] Write a 100-word journal entry each day
- [ ] Use 20+ phrases naturally in conversation
- [ ] Describe a work project clearly using STAR format

---

## Revision: Conversation Role Play

Do this conversation exercise (play both roles):

**Scenario:** You're meeting a colleague from another department at lunch.

Cover these topics in your conversation:
1. Quick greeting / small talk
2. Your current project
3. A challenge you're facing
4. Plans for the weekend
5. Wrap up naturally

Aim for 5 minutes. Record yourself.

---

## Journal Check

Re-read your journal entries from this week. Circle:
- ✅ Sentences you're proud of
- ❌ Sentences with errors

Correct the errors. Count your improvement.

---

## Speaking Task

Record a 3-minute "progress report" on yourself:
> "In the last 4 weeks, I have improved in... I still find [X] difficult. My biggest win this week was... Next week I will focus on..."

This is your real progress marker.
