## The POWER Method

Stop memorizing random word lists. Use this system instead:

| Letter | Step | What to Do |
|--------|------|-----------|
| **P** | Pick | Choose words from **context** (things you actually read/hear) |
| **O** | One word, many forms | Learn the whole **word family** |
| **W** | Write, say, use | Use the word in **3 ways** |
| **E** | Example sentences | Write your **own examples** |
| **R** | Review | Review after 1 day → 3 days → 7 days → 14 days |

---

## Word Families (Learn 1, Know 4)

| Verb | Noun | Adjective | Adverb |
|------|------|-----------|--------|
| communicate | communication | communicative | communicatively |
| manage | management | manageable | — |
| present | presentation | presentable | — |
| organize | organization | organized | — |
| collaborate | collaboration | collaborative | collaboratively |

**The shortcut:** Learn "communicate" + 3 forms = 4 words for the price of 1.

## Hindi Speaker Shortcut: Words You Already Know

Many English business words are already used in Indian offices. **Build on these:**

> meeting, project, deadline, update, report, client, target, team, manager, task, email, presentation, feedback, system, process

You already know 15+ business words. Don't start from zero!

## The 3-Column Notebook Method

Keep a small vocabulary notebook (or note on phone):

| New Word | Hindi Meaning | My Own Example Sentence |
|----------|---------------|------------------------|
| clarify | स्पष्ट करना | "Could you clarify the deadline?" |
| delegate | सौंपना | "I will delegate this task to Ravi." |
| prioritize | प्राथमिकता देना | "We should prioritize the client meeting." |
| escalate | ऊपर भेजना | "I need to escalate this issue to my manager." |
| collaborate | मिलकर काम करना | "Let's collaborate on this project." |

## Daily Vocabulary Target

**5–7 new words per day** (in context, never from random lists)

**Sources to find words:**
- Emails you receive at work
- Articles you read
- Videos you watch
- Meetings you attend

---

## Spaced Repetition Schedule

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| Day 0 | Learn the word |
| Day 1 | Review it once |
| Day 3 | Review it again |
| Day 7 | Quick review |
| Day 14 | Final check |
| Day 30 | Long-term memory locked |

**Best free app:** Anki — create your own flashcards with Hindi meaning + example sentence.

---

## The Context Rule

Never learn a word alone:
- ❌ "Emphasize — जोर देना"
- ✅ "I want to **emphasize** that the deadline is Friday."

The sentence context helps your brain remember the word naturally.
