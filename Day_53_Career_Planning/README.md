## Today's Speaking Challenge

> Speak about your 5-year career plan

---

## Career Talk is a Core Professional Skill

In interviews, reviews, and networking — talking about your goals is essential.

---

## Career Goal Language

**Short-term goals (1–2 years):**
- "In the next year, I'm focused on..."
- "My immediate goal is to..."
- "I'm currently working towards..."

**Medium-term goals (3–5 years):**
- "Over the next few years, I'd like to..."
- "My 5-year plan involves..."
- "I see myself moving towards..."

**Long-term vision:**
- "Ultimately, I want to..."
- "My long-term vision is..."
- "I hope to one day..."

---

## The Career Plan Framework

Build your answer around:

1. **Where I am now:** Current role, skills, experience
2. **Where I want to go:** Next role, industry, specialization
3. **What I'm doing to get there:** Learning, projects, networking
4. **My long-term vision:** Where you want to end up

---

## Sample Career Plan Talk (2 minutes)

> "Currently, I'm working as a [role] and I've been in this field for [X years]. I've built solid skills in [area 1] and [area 2].
>
> In the next 1–2 years, I want to deepen my expertise in [specific area] and take on more [leadership/project/client] responsibilities.
>
> Over the next 5 years, I see myself moving into a [target role] where I can [impact]. I'm actively working on this through [what you're doing — courses, projects, reading].
>
> Long term, I want to [big vision — lead a team, build something, contribute to industry]. I believe the skills I'm developing now are building that foundation."

---

## Speaking Task

Record your own **2-minute career plan talk** using the framework. This is also excellent interview preparation.
