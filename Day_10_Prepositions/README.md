## The Core 6 Prepositions

| Preposition | Hindi | Used For |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| **In** | में | Space inside / Months / Years / Cities |
| **On** | पर | Surfaces / Days / Dates |
| **At** | पर/में | Specific time / Specific place |
| **For** | के लिए | Duration / Purpose |
| **With** | के साथ | Company / Tool |
| **To** | को/की तरफ | Direction / Recipient |

---

## Time Prepositions: The Big 3

This is where Hindi speakers struggle most. Memorize this table:

| Use **In** | Use **On** | Use **At** |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| In the morning | On Monday | At 9 AM |
| In the evening | On Friday | At noon |
| In March | On 15th August | At night |
| In 2024 | On weekdays | At midnight |
| In the afternoon | On my birthday | At 3 PM |

**The rule:**
- **In** = larger period (months, years, parts of day)
- **On** = specific day or date
- **At** = specific clock time

## Place Examples

| In | On | At |
|----|----|----|
| in the office | on the table | at the reception |
| in Delhi | on the floor | at the station |
| in the meeting room | on the wall | at the entrance |

## Office Preposition Practice

Read these sentences and notice the prepositions:

- "The meeting is **at** 3 PM **on** Monday **in** the conference room."
- "I worked **for** 3 hours **with** my team **on** the presentation."
- "Please send this **to** Rahul **by** end of day."
- "She is **in** a call right now."
- "The file is **on** the shared drive."

---

## Common Mistakes

| Wrong | Right |
|-------|-------|
| "I will call you in Monday." | "I will call you **on** Monday." |
| "Meeting is in 3 PM." | "Meeting is **at** 3 PM." |
| "She is working from 2 years." | "She has been working **for** 2 years." |
| "I am at Mumbai." | "I am **in** Mumbai." |
| "He went to home." | "He went **home**." (no preposition!) |

## Practice

Fill in the correct preposition (in / on / at / for / with / to):

1. The conference is _____ Thursday.
2. I will be _____ the office _____ 9 AM.
3. She has worked here _____ 5 years.
4. Please send this report _____ the client.
5. I discussed this _____ my manager.
6. The training is _____ March.

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. on
2. in / at
3. for
4. to
5. with
6. in

</details>

## Speaking Task

Describe where things are **in your office or home** using prepositions. Make 10 sentences. Use at least 5 different prepositions.

Example: "My desk is in the corner. My laptop is on the desk. I keep my notebook at my left. The printer is near the window..."

---

## Key Takeaway

> **In** = larger space/period. **On** = surface/day. **At** = specific point in time or place. These three cause 90% of preposition errors — master them first.
