## Two Present Tenses

### Simple Present = Routine / Habit

- **Formula:** Subject + V1 (add **'s'** for he/she/it)
- **Use:** Something you do regularly

| Example | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| I work every day. | मैं रोज काम करता हूँ। |
| She checks emails in the morning. | वो सुबह emails check करती है। |
| He attends the daily standup. | वो daily standup attend करता है। |

### Present Continuous = Happening RIGHT NOW

- **Formula:** Subject + am/is/are + V**+ing**
- **Use:** Something happening at this exact moment

| Example | Hindi |
|---------|-------|
| I am working right now. | मैं अभी काम कर रहा हूँ। |
| She is attending a call. | वो call attend कर रही है। |
| They are reviewing the report. | वो report review कर रहे हैं। |

---

## The Trick

> If you say **"अभी" (right now)** — use **Continuous**.
> If it's a **habit/routine** — use **Simple**.

| Hindi Clue | English Tense |
|-----------|---------------|
| रोज / हमेशा (every day / always) | Simple Present |
| अभी / इस वक़्त (right now / at this moment) | Present Continuous |

---

## He/She/It Rule (Simple Present Only)

For he/she/it, add **-s** or **-es** to the verb:

| I work | He **works** |
| I go | She **goes** |
| I watch | It **watches** |
| I do | He **does** |

**Common mistake:**
- ❌ "She work in IT"
- ✅ "She **works** in IT"

---

## Practice

Choose Simple Present or Present Continuous:

1. I _____ (work) in Bangalore. *(routine)*
2. She _____ (talk) on the phone right now. *(right now)*
3. He _____ (attend) meetings every Monday. *(routine)*
4. We _____ (finish) the report at the moment. *(right now)*
5. They _____ (send) daily updates. *(routine)*

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. work
2. is talking
3. attends
4. are finishing
5. send

</details>

---

## Speaking Task

Speak 5 sentences about what you do **every day** (Simple Present), then 5 sentences about what you are doing **right now** (Present Continuous).

Record yourself. Notice the difference in how the sentences feel.

---

## Key Takeaway

> **Simple** = habit. **Continuous** = right now. The '-ing' form always needs am/is/are before it.
