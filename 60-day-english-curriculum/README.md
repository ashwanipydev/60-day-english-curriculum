# 60-Day English Curriculum for Hindi Speakers 🇮🇳

A free, practical, structured 60-day English learning curriculum designed specifically for **Hindi-speaking beginner/intermediate learners** who want functional English for **daily life and professional/office communication** — not academic grammar mastery.

> Built for people who want to *use* English confidently — in meetings, emails, conversations, and daily life — not pass a grammar exam.

---

## 🎯 Who is this for?

- Hindi speakers learning English for office/professional use
- Self-learners with 2–3 hours/day to commit for 60 days
- Anyone who wants practical English over grammar theory
- People who want Hindi comparisons and examples to understand faster

## 📘 Curriculum highlights

The main curriculum (`curriculum/60-day-curriculum.md`) includes:

- ✅ Full 60-day roadmap (4 phases: Foundation → Communication → Professional → Fluency)
- ✅ First 15-day grammar foundation plan (only essential grammar, no theory overload)
- ✅ Daily 2.5-hour study timetable
- ✅ Weekly goals for all 8 weeks
- ✅ Speaking practice scripts (introductions, meetings, phone calls, small talk)
- ✅ 50 must-know office English sentences + email templates
- ✅ Vocabulary building strategy (7-layer system)
- ✅ Best free YouTube channels, apps, and websites
- ✅ Movie/series recommendations for learners
- ✅ Common mistakes Hindi speakers make (grammar, pronunciation, vocabulary)
- ✅ Confidence-building exercises (mirror technique, shadowing, mistake log)
- ✅ Final 30-day speaking challenge (one task per day)
- ✅ Progress tracking system

## 📗 Grammar chapters

Deep-dive grammar references, designed with Hindi comparisons and real office examples:

- **[Modal Verbs](grammar/modal-verbs.md)** — can, could, will, would, should, must, may, might, need to
- **[V2 Verb Forms](grammar/v2-verb-forms.md)** — 75 essential past-tense verbs grouped by pattern, with common mistakes and a 7-day study plan

More grammar chapters will be added over time (see [Contributing](#-contributing) below).

## 🚀 How to use this

1. Clone or download this repo
2. Start with `curriculum/60-day-curriculum.md` — follow it day by day
3. Use the `grammar/` chapters as deep-dive references when the curriculum points to them
4. Track your progress using the habit tracker and weekly self-assessment included in the curriculum
5. Don't skip speaking practice — it's included every single day for a reason
