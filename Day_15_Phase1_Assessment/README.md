## Morning Task (1 hour): Written Self-Test

Write these without looking at your notes:

1. **5 sentences about yourself** (present tense, using am/is/are correctly)
2. **5 sentences about yesterday** (past tense, using V2 forms)
3. **5 sentences about your plans** (future tense, using will / going to)
4. **3 polite requests** using modal verbs
5. **3 questions** you would ask in a meeting

---

## Afternoon Task (1 hour): Speaking Assessment

Record yourself answering these questions:

1. "Tell me about yourself." *(aim for 2 minutes)*
2. "What did you do yesterday?"
3. "What are your plans for this week?"
4. "Can you describe your job role?"

---

## How to Grade Yourself

Listen back to your recordings. Count:

| Check | Aim |
|-------|-----|
| Grammar errors per minute | Less than 5 |
| Pauses longer than 3 seconds | Less than 3 per minute |
| Correct use of articles | Most sentences correct |
| S+V+O structure | Almost every sentence |
| Modal verbs used | At least 2–3 times |

**Note:** You're building. Mistakes = progress data. Don't be discouraged — be curious about what you notice.
